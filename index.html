About

Who I am + what you’ll get when the lights go out.

I play a wide range of games — from lighthearted, kid-friendly adventures to brutal, gore-filled nightmares. What pulls me in isn’t just difficulty or shock value — it’s exploration. I love stepping into new worlds, uncovering what they have to offer, and finding what’s hidden in every dark corner.

I’m striving to grow not only as a streamer and gamer, but as a Computer Science student and a parent. Balancing school, streaming, and fatherhood isn’t easy — but it fuels me. I’m learning to design and build with the tools of programming in a way that truly captivates my curiosity and pushes me to evolve.

Gaming isn’t just a hobby for me — it’s a coping mechanism during manic episodes and a way to channel intensity into creativity. Sharing that journey openly is something that brings me real joy. I can’t fully express how much I love the creativity in today’s gaming world in mere words — so instead, I’ll show you the worlds I see… and maybe you’ll begin to see them through my eyes.
